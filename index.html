  • ABOUT INNER FEAR

    Inner Fear - dark metal band from Europe that was formed by Cradle Of Filth drummer Marthus in 1997. At the moment Inner Fear continues its existence after a long hiatus, this time recruiting absolutely new line-up from Europe

    Serg of Latvian dark death metal act Preternatural has taken the position as a vocalist and to fulfill music with beautiful vocal keys Latvian gothic mistress Vic Anselmo also joined the band

    In order to sustaine its metal origins Marthus has involved his long-standing friend and guitarist Ashok (Root) with Khopec from Czech Republic. Also band decided to involve Serhio from technical death scalpel Neglected Fields that was originally established in Latvia

    Currently the band is making preproduction for the already recorded new album, as well as working on the brand new songs
